Step 1 of 4 — Scan the beneficiary's ration card QR code

📌 Instructions: Click "Open Webcam Scanner" below. When the webcam opens, hold the QR code ration card in front of the camera. The system will automatically detect and fetch family details.

📋 How It Works · எவ்வாறு செயல்படுகிறது

1
Click Scanner Button

Press the button to activate your laptop webcam.

2
Show QR Code

Hold the printed QR ration card in front of the camera clearly.

3
Auto Detection

System reads card ID and fetches all family members automatically.

4
Proceed to Selection

You will be redirected to select the visiting member.

⚠ Note: Ensure good lighting while scanning. If QR code is not detected within 30 seconds, press ESC and try again. Card must be undamaged and clearly printed.
